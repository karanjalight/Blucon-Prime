ABOUT US


We pivot your business to the next level

Blucon Consulting Ltd is a Pan-African advisory and management consulting firm that provides solutions in the fields of Project Management, Strategy, Research, Marketing and business sustainability solutions.

Blucon Consulting prides itself in understanding the rigors of entrepreneurship. We have built invaluable expertise in maneuvering business undertakings from idea stages to actualization. In the Coast region particularly, we have pivoted to become the premier referral partner for entrepreneurial penetration into existing industries. With numerous opportunities in the Blue Economy, Special Economic Zones, Shipping, Agriculture and Manufacturing, BluCon Consulting offers to walk with you from process conceptualization, to the technicalities of licensing, registration, capital planning and eventual business set-up. We believe in collaborative partnerships that offers professional foresighted execution across all stages of business.

We don't believe in a one-size-fits-all approach to Consultancy. Our aim is to transform businesses from th inside, through offering cutting edge client specific solutions. We draw insight from client data, building flexible, future-oriented and resilient businesses
